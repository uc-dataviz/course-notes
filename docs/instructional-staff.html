2 Instructional staff

2.1 Instructor

Dr. Benjamin Soltoff is Assistant Senior Instructional Professor in Computational Social Science & the College, as well as the Associate Director of the Masters in Computational Social Science program at the University of Chicago. He is a political scientist with concentrations in American government, political methodology, and law and courts. Additionally, he has training and experience in data science, big data analytics, and policy evaluation. He currently teaches courses in social scientific research design, computational modeling, data science, math/statistics, and data visualization.

Benjamin’s office hours are Tuesdays 12:30-2:30pm in room 219 in 1155 E 60th St (across the hall from the MACSS student lounge).
